Our Journey

Borneo MMA Gym was founded by four passionate martial artists Larry, Evan, Fathurahman, and Dylan. The vision started during a friendly sparring session in a local community center in Kuching. Realizing the lack of high-quality, inclusive martial arts training in the region, they joined forces to build a space where fighters of all backgrounds could train, grow, and compete. By 2020, the dream became a reality with the grand opening of Borneo MMA Gym.

Professional Fighter Stats

Over 20 professional fighters have trained here since our founding. Our alumni have competed in ONE Championship, Road to UFC, and regional tournaments across Southeast Asia, amassing:

  • 120+ wins in MMA, Muay Thai, and Boxing
  • 30 submission victories
  • 15 championship titles won

Facilities

MMA Cage

A full-size MMA cage used for sparring and competition simulation. Built to regulation standards for safety and performance.

Boxing Ring

Classic elevated boxing ring used in striking-focused training and amateur fights. Perfect for footwork and ring awareness drills.

BJJ Mat

High-quality grappling mats for Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu, wrestling, and ground technique drilling. Easy on joints, built for rolling.
