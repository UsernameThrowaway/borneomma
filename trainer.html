Meet Our Trainers

Coach Evan

Muay Thai Fighter

Coming from a boxing background, Evan transitioned into Muay Thai and found his rhythm. A veteran of local and international tournaments including ONE Championship, he holds a professional record of 13-3-0. Known for his grit and heart, his motto is: “Losing is a process of improvement.”

Coach Larry

Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu

A BJJ black belt, Coach Larry has competed in ADCC, IBJJF, and MGL. Mentored by names like Craig Jones and Charles Oliveira, he brings unmatched submission knowledge to Borneo MMA.

Coach Fathurahman

Boxing Coach

A former WBO Middleweight Champion, Coach Fathurahman trained in the legendary Mayweather Gym. With an undefeated 10-0 coaching record, he now guides new generations of fighters at Borneo MMA.

Coach Dylan

Mixed Martial Artist

A hybrid fighter and active pro, Coach Dylan has competed in Bellator, Cage Warriors, and even StreetBeefs. A student of multiple disciplines, he balances his own career with coaching rising talent at Borneo MMA.
